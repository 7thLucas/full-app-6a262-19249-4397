# KONX — InterviewLens

## Product
InterviewLens is a web application that analyzes audio recordings of interview sessions — job interviews, user research interviews, and journalistic or investigative interviews — and surfaces structured intelligence from spoken content. Users upload or record audio; the app transcribes, analyzes, and returns a rich breakdown of the conversation.

## Tagline
Turn interview recordings into honest, actionable insight.

## The Problem
Interview sessions produce unstructured, ephemeral data. Hiring managers listen to recordings once, take scattered notes, and make decisions from memory. UX researchers transcribe manually or pay for costly services and still struggle to surface patterns across sessions. Key signals — hesitation, sentiment shifts, topic gaps, repeated themes — go undetected. Critical decisions rest on gut feel rather than evidence.

## Core Features
- **Audio upload and recording** — upload MP3/WAV/M4A files or record directly in the browser.
- **Auto-transcription** — fast, accurate speech-to-text with speaker labels and timestamps.
- **Sentiment analysis** — per-speaker, per-segment emotional tone charted over time.
- **Topic extraction** — key themes ranked by frequency and importance.
- **Talk-time analytics** — visual ratio breakdown; flags sessions where interviewer talked more than subject.
- **Filler and hesitation detection** — counts of "um", "uh", pauses, restarts per speaker.
- **Session summary** — AI-generated executive summary with top 3–5 highlights.
- **Export and sharing** — PDF report and shareable link with access control.

## Target Users
1. **HR managers** — evaluate candidates consistently and reduce bias in post-interview reviews.
2. **UX researchers** — synthesize findings across multiple sessions without manual transcription.
3. **Journalists and investigators** — surface key claims, contradictions, and topic threads from long-form interviews.

## Pricing
- **Free** — 3 sessions/month, up to 30 min each.
- **Pro** — $19/mo, unlimited sessions up to 2 hours each.
- **Team** — $79/mo, shared workspace, 5 seats.

## Strategic Principles
- Privacy first: audio processed then deleted; transcripts encrypted at rest.
- Speed matters: first analysis results in under 60 seconds for a 30-minute session.
- Actionable output: every metric shown must link to a decision a user can make.

## Brand & Tone
Calm, precise, trustworthy. Professional without being corporate; intelligent without being overwhelming.
