# Product Overview

## Product Name
InterviewLens

## Tagline
Turn interview recordings into honest, actionable insight.

## What It Is
InterviewLens is a web application that analyzes audio recordings of interview sessions — job interviews, user research interviews, and journalistic or investigative interviews — and surfaces structured intelligence from the spoken content. Users upload or record audio; the app transcribes, analyzes, and returns a rich breakdown of the conversation.

## The Problem
Interview sessions produce unstructured, ephemeral data. Hiring managers listen to recordings once, take scattered notes, and make decisions from memory. UX researchers transcribe manually or pay for costly services and still struggle to surface patterns across sessions. Key signals — hesitation, sentiment shifts, topic gaps, repeated themes — go undetected. Critical decisions (who to hire, which insight to act on) rest on gut feel rather than evidence.

## The Solution
InterviewLens processes interview audio automatically and returns:
- A full, timestamped transcript
- Speaker diarization (who spoke when)
- Sentiment arc across the session
- Key topic and theme extraction
- Talk-time ratio (interviewer vs. candidate/subject)
- Hesitation and filler-word detection
- Auto-generated session summary with highlight moments
- Exportable report for sharing with stakeholders

## Target Users
1. **HR managers** — evaluate candidates consistently and reduce bias in post-interview reviews; upload recordings via the dashboard.
2. **UX researchers** — synthesize findings across multiple sessions without manual transcription effort.
3. **Journalists and investigators** — surface key claims, contradictions, and topic threads from long-form interviews.

## Core Features
- **Audio upload and recording** — upload MP3/WAV/M4A files or record directly in the browser.
- **Auto-transcription** — fast, accurate speech-to-text with speaker labels.
- **Sentiment analysis** — per-speaker, per-segment emotional tone charted over time.
- **Topic extraction** — key themes ranked by frequency and importance.
- **Talk-time analytics** — visual ratio breakdown; flag sessions where the interviewer talked more than the subject.
- **Filler and hesitation detection** — counts of "um", "uh", pauses, restarts per speaker.
- **Session summary** — AI-generated executive summary with top 3–5 highlights.
- **Export and sharing** — PDF report and shareable link with access control.
- **Analysis history** — the dashboard stores every uploaded recording and its complete analysis results; users can browse, search, and re-open any past session from a persistent library in the Analysis section. Each session detail view uses a two-column layout: the left column shows all analysis results (sentiment arc, topic extraction, talk-time ratio, filler detection, session summary, and export options); the right column shows the original audio/video recording (playable inline) and the full timestamped transcript.

## Positioning
Premium, privacy-first audio intelligence for professionals who run structured interviews. Not a generic transcription tool — built for the interview workflow end to end.

## Tone and Brand
Calm, precise, trustworthy. Clean interface with editorial data design. Subdued navy and slate palette with a warm amber accent. Professional without being corporate; intelligent without being overwhelming.

## Pricing Model
Freemium: free tier (3 sessions/month, up to 30 min each), Pro tier ($19/mo, unlimited sessions up to 2 hours each), Team tier ($79/mo, shared workspace, 5 seats).

## Strategic Principles
- Privacy first: audio is processed and then deleted; transcripts encrypted at rest.
- Speed matters: first analysis results in under 60 seconds for a 30-minute session.
- Actionable output: every metric shown must link to a decision a user can make.
